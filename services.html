Parking Lot Design Services


Every parking lot is unique and at NXN Infra, we understand that. Our experienced engineers and field experts create custom plans as per your specific needs.

We consider everything such as the type of traffic using your lot, the weight of vehicles, how it looks to customers, the slope for drainage, and even the weather in your area. Things like heavy trucks, poor drainage, high traffic, and harsh climates can all damage pavement.

We are experts at designing and building parking lots and garages. Our team thinks about everything like how cars get in and out, how easy it is to move around inside and making sure the design works well for everyone who uses it.

Our goal is to provide you with a long-lasting and well-maintained parking lot that functions efficiently. We focus on quality materials, smart construction, and the right maintenance plan to keep your pavement in good condition for years to come.

Asphalt Maintenance


Taking care of your asphalt pavement will extend its life and save you money in the long run. Regular maintenance like patching holes and filling cracks prevents bigger problems from developing. Sealcoating also adds a protective layer.

When your asphalt starts showing wear and tear, resurfacing is a great way to restore it. This involves adding a new layer of asphalt usually about 1.5 to 2 inches thick. Milling and paving is another option where we remove the top layer before adding new asphalt.

Our experts will assess your pavement's condition, determine the causes of any damage, and recommend the best solutions for long-term durability.As we are professionals, we are committed to making sure that your pavement is in good condition. We work hard to provide excellent service.

Parking Lot Construction


A strong foundation is vital for a long-lasting asphalt parking lot. We start by checking the existing soil which ensures it is compacted correctly, drains well, and is thick enough to support the asphalt.

For new construction or complete replacements, we handle everything from demolition and removal to building from scratch. Our experienced team of field superintendents oversees every step which guarantees top-notch quality and safety.
